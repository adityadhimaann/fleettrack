About Us

With a proud 30-year history, we are meticulous custodians of your vessels and cargo.

Our History

For over three decades, Fleet Management Limited has been at the forefront of the maritime industry, setting standards for excellence in ship management and maritime services. Founded in 1994, we have grown to become one of the world's largest independent third-party ship management companies.

Our journey began with a vision to transform the ship management industry through innovation, dedication to quality, and an unwavering commitment to our people. Today, we manage a diverse fleet of over 650 vessels, supported by a global network of more than 27,000 seafarers and 1,200 shore-based professionals.

Global Presence

With 24 offices across 10 countries, we maintain a strong global presence that enables us to provide comprehensive support to our clients worldwide. Our strategic locations ensure we can respond quickly and effectively to our clients' needs, no matter where they are.

Our global network is supported by state-of-the-art technology and infrastructure, allowing us to maintain the highest standards of service delivery across all regions.
