World-Class Fleet Management Services

With the support of our teams of global professionals, we are shaping the future of ship management, ensuring that the goods and materials powering global economies are delivered swiftly and securely to their destinations.

We are custodians of our clients' vessels and cargo, looking after them as if they were our own. A combination of experience, reliability, innovation and sheer passion have enabled us to become one of the world's leading providers of ship management services.

Three main attributes form the core of our business: an unparalleled commitment to our people both onshore and at sea, client partnerships led by integrity and transparency, and a commitment to building the most innovative ship management company in the world.

People: Empowering Our Global Workforce

Seafaring is in our DNA. From our crews to our Chairman, maritime trade is what drives us. It is a passion shared by our commitment to the 27,000 seafarers and 1,200 shore-based colleagues we have working across 24 offices in 10 countries.

Our talent development programmes provide comprehensive training, education, and mentoring opportunities, to equip our seafarers and employees with the skills needed to navigate an industry marked by digitalisation, automation, and regulatory requirements.

Partnerships: Tailored Solutions for Long-Term Success

We recognise that our role now extends beyond traditional ship management; our clients need partners and innovators as we navigate increasingly complex industry challenges together.

The size of our operations – with more than 650+ ships under Technical Management and New Building Supervision services – provides us with the insight to identify trends early, enabling us to offer our clients fresh perspectives and solutions that anticipate emerging needs.

Performance: Driving Global Economic Growth with Operational Excellence

With a 240+ strong technical and operations team, we maintain vessels to the highest standards, ensuring compliance and operational excellence. From routine maintenance to managing complex technical challenges, we handle every aspect of vessel operations.

Our focus on proactive maintenance helps reduce downtime while increasing efficiency and reliability, and delivering the performance our clients expect.
